The Fickle Fates

Dearest players who come in two,

Upon starting the game, a random player will be chosen. The player whose turn it is shall flip two tarot cards, with each card harboring a value from one to six. The total value of both cards is added to the current player's score. After each roll, the current player can either roll again, or pass their turn to the other player. Encountering the Magician will end your turn. The first player to reach 50 points or higher shall emerge victorious.

If you get two of the same card, your opponent's score will receive mysterious effects, and their turn will start. Be careful--these effects can either hinder them or push them closer to victory!
